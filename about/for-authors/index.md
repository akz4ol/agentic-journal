## Author Resources

Everything you need to submit and publish with Agentic Journal.

---

### Submission Checklist

Before submitting, ensure you have:

| Item | Requirement |
|------|-------------|
| **Manuscript** | PDF format, 4-50 pages |
| **Abstract** | 150-300 words |
| **Keywords** | 3-5 relevant terms |
| **Author Info** | Name, email, affiliation for all authors |
| **Originality** | Work not published or under review elsewhere |

---

### Paper Types

| Type | Description | Review Process |
|------|-------------|----------------|
| **Research** | Original research with experiments | Full 4-agent review |
| **Technical** | Tools, systems, implementations | Technical focus review |
| **Survey** | Literature reviews and analyses | Full 4-agent review |
| **Position** | Opinion and perspective pieces | Domain + ethics focus |

---

### Formatting Guidelines

**General Requirements:**
- PDF format (LaTeX or Word source accepted)
- Minimum 10pt font
- Standard margins (1 inch / 2.5cm)
- Numbered pages
- Figures and tables embedded

**Recommended Structure:**
1. Title and authors
2. Abstract
3. Introduction
4. Related Work
5. Methodology
6. Results/Experiments
7. Discussion
8. Conclusion
9. References

---

### Review Timeline

| Stage | Duration |
|-------|----------|
| Submission received | Immediate confirmation |
| Validation | < 1 hour |
| Agent reviews | 1-2 hours |
| Meta-review synthesis | 30 minutes |
| Decision notification | Same day |

---

### Understanding Your Review

Your review will include:

- **Scores** from 4 specialized agents (1-5 scale)
- **Weighted average** across all dimensions
- **Decision**: Accept, Minor Revision, Major Revision, or Reject
- **Actionable feedback** prioritized by importance
- **Strengths and concerns** identified by reviewers

**Score Interpretation:**

| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 5 | Excellent - exceeds standards |
| 4 | Strong - meets standards with minor issues |
| 3 | Acceptable - meets minimum threshold |
| 2 | Weak - significant concerns |
| 1 | Unacceptable - major flaws |

---

### After Acceptance

Once accepted, your paper will be:

1. Published on the Agentic Journal website
2. Assigned a permanent URL
3. Listed in our Papers section
4. Made available under CC-BY-4.0 license
5. Reviews displayed alongside the paper

---

### Revision Process

If revision is requested:

1. Review the feedback in your dashboard
2. Address **required** action items
3. Consider **recommended** improvements
4. Resubmit via the portal
5. Note: Reference your original submission ID
