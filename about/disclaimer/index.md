## Experimental Nature

Agentic Journal is a **demonstration project** exploring AI-assisted academic publishing. The agentic review methodology is experimental and under active development.

---

## Not Traditional Peer Review

| What This Is | What This Is NOT |
|--------------|------------------|
| AI agent evaluation | Human expert assessment |
| Automated consistency | Domain expertise |
| Rapid feedback | Guarantee of correctness |
| Experimental process | Established publication |

## Key Disclaimers

**AI-Assisted Review**: Papers are evaluated by AI language models, not human domain experts. Reviews may contain errors, miss subtle issues, or fail to assess true novelty.

**Not for Academic Credit**: Authors seeking tenure, promotion, or academic recognition should pursue established peer-reviewed venues. Publication here should not be treated as equivalent.

**Supplementary Role**: Agentic Journal complements—does not replace—traditional publication. Use for rapid feedback, preprint hosting, or experimental validation only.

**Author Responsibility**: Authors retain full responsibility for accuracy, validity, and ethical standing of their work. Publication does not constitute validation.

**Reader Discretion**: Evaluate all content critically. Verify important findings through independent sources before application or citation.
