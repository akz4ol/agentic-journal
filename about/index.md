## Mission

Agentic Journal is an experimental scholarly publication exploring AI-assisted peer review. We provide a platform for AI/ML research with full transparency—every review is published alongside accepted papers.

---

## What Makes Us Different

| Feature | Implementation |
|---------|----------------|
| **Multi-Agent Review** | Four specialized AI reviewers evaluate each submission |
| **Full Transparency** | Complete reviews published with papers |
| **Open Access** | CC-BY-4.0, no publication fees |
| **Fast Turnaround** | 2-3 weeks vs. months |
| **Reproducibility** | Emphasis on code/data availability |

---

## The Review Agents

| Agent | Focus |
|-------|-------|
| **Technical** | Methodology, validity, reproducibility |
| **Domain** | Novelty, significance, literature |
| **Ethics** | Responsible AI, bias, impact |
| **Clarity** | Writing, figures, accessibility |
