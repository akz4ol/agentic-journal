- The work is original and has not been published elsewhere

- Reviews are generated by AI agents (Claude by Anthropic)

- Human oversight is limited

All accepted papers are published under **Creative Commons CC-BY-4.0**, meaning:

- Anyone can share and adapt the work
- Attribution must be given to the authors
- No additional restrictions may be applied

### 4. Data and Privacy

- Email addresses are used only for submission communication
- We do not sell or share personal data with third parties

### 5. Review Transparency

- Authors may request review redaction in exceptional circumstances
- Review scores and rationales are displayed alongside papers
